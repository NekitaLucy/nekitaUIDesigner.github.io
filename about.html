About

Nekita Lucy Surman

Hello, I'm Nekita Surman, a UX/UI designer specializing in UI design. What sets me apart is my 10 years of experience in customer service and communications. I have a human-centred approach to UI design. As a designer, I am passionate about creating experiences that provide functional value and are loved by the users.

Why I'm passionate about UI

I am passionate about UI Design because it merges the realms of creativity and functionality in a harmonious way. I enjoy crafting visually stunning and intuitive interfaces that enhance user experiences. UI Design enables me to blend my artistic flair with problem-solving skills.

Origin Story

I was born in Seychelles, spent most of my childhood in Rwanda, and spent my formative years in Montreal, Canada, where I currently reside. It was here that I discovered my passion for art, culture, and creativity.

Throughout my nomadic journey, I have developed strong adaptability skills. I have learned to embrace change, navigate unfamiliar territories, and forge connections with people from diverse backgrounds. These experiences have cultivated my resilience, flexibility, and open-mindedness, enabling me to approach new challenges with curiosity and enthusiasm.
