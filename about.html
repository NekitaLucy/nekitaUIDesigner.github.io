About Me

Nekita Lucy Surman

Hello, I'm a UX/UI Designer specializing in UI design. I'm currently transitioning careers from an information agent to a UI designer. What sets me apart from other designers is my ten years of experience in customer service and communications. I'm passionate about creating experiences that provide functional value and are loved by users, I take a human-centred approach to UI design.

Why I'm passionate about UI

My creative journey began with my hobby of drawing, painting, and digital art, as well as my fascination with animation as a visual storytelling medium.

The UI/UX design world is a perfect culmination of practicality and imagination. It integrates creativity and functionality perfectly, and I emulate that in my everyday life and work.

Creatively, I thrive in an environment where I'm working on multiple projects, while on the functional side, I enjoy having an efficient interface to track my goals which I use daily. I've always experimented with new tools, including bullet journaling, Notion, Google Sheets, and AI. Having a system to track my progress motivates me.

Origin Story

I was born in Seychelles, spent most of my childhood in Rwanda, and my formative years in Montreal, Canada, where I currently reside. It was here that I discovered my passion for art, culture, and creativity.

Throughout my nomadic journey, I developed strong adaptability skills. I learned to embrace change, navigate unfamiliar territories, and forge connections with people from diverse backgrounds. These experiences helped me cultivate resilience, flexibility, and open-mindedness to approach new challenges with curiosity and enthusiasm.
